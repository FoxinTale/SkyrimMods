Name: Aileen Zoey Samson.
Meaning (roughly): Light, Life and the power of the Sun.

Age: Approx 22.
Gender: Female.
Species: Anthro Bipedal Alicorn. (Unicorn and Pegasus).

Height: 4'11" / 150 Cm.

Build: Petite. Not super slim, but not frail either for her size. Fairly athletic.

Appearance:
Light blue coat that is almost peach fuzz in appearance. Large purple eyes, and a pastel pink/purple striped mane and tail.
Soft freckles around her cheeks. Small wings that sit on her upper back. Her wings are too undersized to support physics based flight, but are perfectly capable of magic assisted flight.

Personality:
Aileen leans towards INFP. 
She tries to be kind and gentle, with some of her curiosity shining through at times. Tries to be easily approachable , and can be timid at times. She also dislikes confrontation, and taking up space.
Her voice is calm, reassuring and almost childlike in nature.

She enjoys quiet afternoons, spending time with friends and sitting in warm sunlight.


Magic:
This is what sets her apart from most. Aileen was born with a rather unusual condition. She knows ordinary magic, utilitiy, healing, but has no formal training aside from magical flight training.
Her family decided to not continue advanced testing to figure out her condition after it was determined it wasn't having any negative effect on her wellbeing.

Her condition, while not named, the meaining of her name hints at it. She is, simply a mortal being born with the power of a sun goddess who wants to have a quiet life.
It is characterised by incredibly high magical output and stamina. Stable regulation and impressive endurance.
